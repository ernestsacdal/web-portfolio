## Overview

PreTriage is an AI-first patient intake platform designed to streamline hospital emergency triage workflows. Patients complete a guided symptom assessment before arrival, allowing staff to prepare and prioritize care in advance.

## Problem

Emergency rooms face growing patient volume with static staff capacity. Traditional triage is done on-site, creating bottlenecks at the exact moment care is needed most.

## Solution

A conversational AI intake flow that collects structured symptom data, assigns a triage priority score, and surfaces a pre-populated patient card to ER staff before the patient walks in.

## Technical Architecture

- **Frontend:** Next.js App Router with streaming UI for the intake conversation
- **AI Layer:** OpenAI GPT-4o with function calling for structured symptom extraction
- **Database:** Supabase with row-level security per hospital tenant
- **Real-time:** Supabase Realtime for live patient queue updates on staff dashboards

## Outcome

Deployed across 3 pilot clinics. Average check-in time reduced by 40%. Staff reported higher confidence in patient readiness scores.
